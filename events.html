Upcoming Events at Topsail Beach

Annual Topsail Beach Sandcastle Contest

Date: July 15th, 2024

Location: Topsail Beach

Description: Join us for the annual sandcastle contest where participants of all ages showcase their creativity and craftsmanship. Prizes will be awarded for various categories.

Cost: Free for spectators. Participation fees may apply for contestants.

Live Music Nights at Surfside Bar & Grill

Date: Every Friday from 7:00 PM

Location: Surfside Bar & Grill

Description: Enjoy live music performances by local artists while savoring delicious food and drinks at Surfside Bar & Grill. A perfect way to unwind and kickstart your weekend.

Cost: Free admission. Prices vary for food and drinks.

Guided Birdwatching Tours

Date: Every Saturday morning

Location: Topsail Island Nature Preserve

Description: Join experienced birdwatching guides for a leisurely stroll through Topsail Island Nature Preserve, home to a diverse range of bird species. Learn about local bird habitats and behaviors while enjoying the beauty of nature.

Cost: $25 per person for guided tour.

Sunset Yoga Sessions

Date: Every Wednesday evening

Location: Beachfront Yoga Studio

Description: Unwind and rejuvenate with sunset yoga sessions held on the picturesque beachfront of Topsail Beach. Led by certified instructors, these sessions offer a blend of relaxation and physical wellness against the backdrop of stunning coastal views.

Cost: $15 per person per session.

Topsail Island Food Festival

Date: October 5th-7th, 2024

Location: Topsail Beach Community Center

Description: Indulge in a gastronomic journey at the Topsail Island Food Festival, featuring a variety of culinary delights from local chefs and vendors. Enjoy live music, cooking demonstrations, and food competitions amidst a vibrant festival atmosphere.

Cost: Admission is free. Food and drink purchases vary.
