Activities at Topsail Beach

Beachcombing and Shell Hunting

Explore the sandy shores of Topsail Beach and discover a variety of seashells, sand dollars, and other treasures washed ashore. Perfect for leisurely walks along the coastline.

Cost: Free

Fishing Charters

Experience the thrill of deep-sea fishing with professional fishing charters available at Topsail Beach. Whether you're a novice angler or an experienced fisherman, there's something for everyone.

Cost: Prices vary depending on charter and duration

Water Sports

Indulge in a range of exciting water sports activities such as surfing, paddleboarding, and kayaking along the pristine coastline of Topsail Beach. Rentals and guided tours are available for enthusiasts of all skill levels.

Cost: Rental prices start at $25 per hour. Guided tours range from $50 to $100 per person.

Nature Trails and Wildlife Watching

Embark on scenic nature trails winding through the lush landscapes of Topsail Island, offering glimpses of diverse ecosystems and abundant wildlife. Guided tours led by experienced naturalists provide insightful commentary on the flora and fauna.

Cost: Some trails may have entrance fees ranging from $5 to $15 per person. Guided tours typically range from $20 to $50 per person.
